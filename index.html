How We Work

Our Process

We've refined a clear, transparent process that keeps you in the loop and your project on track — every step of the way.

01

Free Consultation

We visit your home, listen to your vision, take measurements, and understand your timeline and budget before anything else.

02

Detailed Proposal

You receive a clear scope of work, material selections, timeline, and all-in pricing — no vague estimates, no hidden charges.

03

Build & Updates

We get to work. You receive regular updates, have a direct line to your project lead, and can check progress anytime.
